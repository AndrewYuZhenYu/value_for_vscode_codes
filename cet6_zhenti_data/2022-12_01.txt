 Writing ( 30 minutes)
Directions: For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write an essay that begins with the sentence “With the
application of information technology in education, college students can now learn in more diverse and efficient
ways.” You can make statements, give reasons, or cite examples to develop your essay. You should write at least 150
words but no more than 200 words.
