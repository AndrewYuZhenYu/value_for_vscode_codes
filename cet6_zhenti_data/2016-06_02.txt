2016 年 6 月大学英语六级考试真题（第二套）
Part I Writing (30 minutes)
For this part, you are allowed 30 minutes to write a short essay on e-learning. Try to imagine what will happen when
more and more people study online instead of attending school. You are required to write at least 150 words but no
more than 200 words.

Answer Sheet 1 with a single line through the centre.
